Once again, welcome to my personal portfolio website! My name is Miroslav Tkačik and I am aspiring, selftaught frontend web developer. I'm 21 years old and I come from small village in the east of Slovakia, though I'm living in the Czech Republic at the moment.

I have been learning web development for 9 months now, with few short breaks. Mostly focused on 3 pillars of frontend development - HTML, CSS and Javascript. Along the way I also got some experience in some additional tools, for instance CSS framework Bootstrap, CSS preprocessor SASS or JavaScript frameworks jQuery and Typescript. Right now I'm trying to master Vue.js, getting familiar with WordPress and maybe even basics of Adobe Photoshop. I also have small experience with GitHub, though I think that the real versioning experience will come once I get to work on some real world projects with group of people. My next steps will probably lead to some backend programming languages PHP, Node.js or C#, plus some database language. However, my future is highly flexible according to my future employer's needs. Even though I haven't worked on any real world project I am prepared to embark upon my programming journey and work hard to achieve my one and only goal - becoming true developer!
